# Product brief

## Working name

TechCachet Audit & Risk Insights for Jira

## Positioning

Detect queue risks before they become SLA breaches, escalations, or audit problems.

## Target users

- Jira admins
- Service desk managers
- Operations leads
- Compliance-minded internal support teams

## MVP promise

Given a JQL query or saved filter, the app returns a prioritized feed of actionable insights:

- overdue work
- SLA risk
- stale in-progress work
- aging work in status

## Differentiation

Most Jira apps emphasize charts and reporting. This app emphasizes:

- insight-first summaries
- operational prioritization
- one-click drill-down into affected issues

## MVP exclusions

- AI-generated recommendations
- notifications
- external APIs
- long historical reporting
- cross-instance aggregation
